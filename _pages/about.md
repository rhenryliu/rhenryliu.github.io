Hi! I am a cosmologist and a fifth-year Ph.D. Candidate in the UC Berkeley Physics Department advised by Uroš Seljak and Simone Ferraro. I spend a lot of my time being confused and working on aspects of baryon feedback, utilising different cosmological surveys and observables, simulations, and statistical methods. My work is supported by the NSERC Postgraduate Scholarship - Doctoral Fellowship.
